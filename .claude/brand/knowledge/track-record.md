# Law Enforcement & Track Record

## Criminal Accountability Program
Assure DeFi® maintains active partnerships with law enforcement agencies to pursue criminals who defraud verified projects and their investors.

## Law Enforcement Partnerships
Active collaboration with:
- **FBI** - Federal Bureau of Investigation
- **SEC** - Securities and Exchange Commission
- **CFTC** - Commodity Futures Trading Commission
- **IRS CI** - Internal Revenue Service Criminal Investigation
- **Interpol** - International Criminal Police Organization
- Various international law enforcement bodies
- **US Attorney's Office** - Federal prosecutors

## Investigation Protocol
When fraud is suspected:
1. **Evidence Crowdsourcing** - Gather information from affected investors
2. **Forensic Investigation** - Fund and conduct blockchain forensics
3. **Intelligence Reports** - Generate detailed reports for authorities
4. **Authority Cooperation** - Full assistance to law enforcement
5. **Investor Support** - Guide victims through reporting process

## Fraud Protection Policy
- Nationality disclosure of verified individuals upon suspected fraud
- Full verification file release to authorities when legally compelled
- Active support for investor complaints and legal action
- No obstruction of legitimate investigations

## Notable Case: Gray Digital (2024)
- **Situation**: Investment fund collected $42M from approximately 1,000 investors promising 200% annual returns
- **Assure's Action**: Conducted pro bono investigation after concerned investors reached out
- **Finding**: Identified $8.4 million shortfall between funds collected and returned
- **Response**: Issued public report advising investors to contact authorities
- **Outcome**: SEC subpoenaed Assure DeFi® for investigation documents; Assure fully cooperated and provided all files
- **Source**: DL News (November 2024)
